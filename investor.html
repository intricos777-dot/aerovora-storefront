The Problem

Bee colonies are collapsing — 40% loss annually in the US. At the same time, agriculture still relies on broadcast chemical spraying: 100% coverage, <5% hits the target. Farmers need per-plant precision, not blanket treatments.

The Solution

AV-1 Apis — autonomous hexacopter with 5 interchangeable payload modules. It pollinates, monitors, sprays, and samples every plant individually. NVIDIA Jetson Orin onboard, 35-min flight time, 5G swarm mesh. No human pilot needed.
